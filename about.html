Attendance Tracking System

The Attendance Tracking System is designed to streamline the process of monitoring attendance in schools, universities, and organizations. It provides real-time tracking and generates comprehensive reports for both administrators and users. It also includes details of all students along with subject notes, previous year question papers, routines, and syllabi for easy access by students and teachers.

Key Criteria:

  • Automated attendance marking through biometric or RFID systems.
  • Manual override option for attendance correction by administrators.
  • Integration with academic portals for seamless data management.
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly attendance report generation.
  • Real-time alerts for absenteeism or irregularities.
  • Support for mobile and web-based platforms.
